1. Summary

  • The App provides Tamil calendar, panchangam, and related spiritual content.
  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise GPS location.
  • The App uses third-party services for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, and app updates.
  • Calendar data may be stored locally on your device and fetched from our servers when online.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

The App does not ask you to register or submit personal profile information. Any city or date selections you make inside the App are used only to display calendar and panchangam content and are not linked to a user identity.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the App, we and our service providers may automatically collect:

  • Device and app information — such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, and general device settings.
  • Usage and analytics data — such as screens viewed, app opens, feature interactions, and session duration (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash and diagnostic data — such as error logs and stack traces to help us fix bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Advertising identifiers — such as Google Advertising ID (Android) or Identifier for Advertisers (iOS), used to show and measure ads.
  • Network information — such as IP address and connection type, which may be processed by our servers and third-party ad/analytics providers.

2.3 Information we do not collect

The App does not request access to:

  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts, SMS, or call logs
  • Photo library or external storage (beyond the App’s own local files)

Panchangam times are calculated for a selected or default city (for example, Chennai). This is not the same as collecting your real-time physical location.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve calendar, panchangam, and related App features
  • Display banner, native, and interstitial advertisements
  • Measure App performance and understand how features are used
  • Detect, diagnose, and fix crashes or technical issues
  • Check whether a newer App version is required (via Firebase Remote Config)
  • Comply with applicable law and enforce our terms

4. Local Storage on Your Device

To support offline use, the App may copy bundled calendar data (such as a local SQLite database) to your device’s application storage. This data contains public calendar and panchangam information, not personal information about you.

The App may also read its own app version through system APIs to compare against minimum supported versions when checking for updates.

5. Network Requests

When the App is online, it may communicate with our backend API to fetch calendar content. Requests may include non-personal parameters such as city identifier and date. We do not attach your identity to these requests.

The App requires an internet connection for ads, analytics, crash reporting, remote configuration, and online calendar sync. An offline mode may be available in some builds using locally stored data.

7. Advertising and Your Choices

The App is supported by advertising. Ad partners may use advertising identifiers and other technical data to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

You can limit personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

You may still see non-personalized ads after changing these settings. Ads can be disabled only by not using the App or by using a build where ads are turned off.
